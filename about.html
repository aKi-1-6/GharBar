GharBar is an event venue specifically designed to provide independent artists with a platform to showcase their performances. It is a community-based venue that aims to support and promote emerging and underrepresented artists in various fields of art such as music, dance, poetry, theater and more.

GharBar provides a space for artists to come together and share their talents with audiences. This can help independent artists gain exposure and build a following, as well as allow audiences to discover new and exciting performances. GharBar is also working on providing an online platform for these artists to showcase their performances and make it accessible for wider audience and also have it recorded for future use.

GharBar provides an opportunity for the local community to experience and support the work of independent artists, fostering an appreciation for the arts and promoting cultural diversity. It may also serve as a launching point for independent artists to take the next step in their careers. GharBar would also provide workshops and training for artists to further enhance their skills, which would further benefit the independent artists community.

GharBar is an event venue that aims to support and promote independent artists by providing them with a platform to showcase their performances and also give them opportunities to train and network to benefit their careers.
